Aatrox - The Darkin Blade

Aatrox is an AD bruiser that deals AoE damage with his abilities and drain tanks off of built in omnivamp. Champions that Aatrox beat are usually immobile melee or ranged champions, who are unable to avoid his E-Q combo. Champions that beat Aatrox are ones that can easily dodge this E-Q combo, which severly reduces Aatrox's healing and damage.
